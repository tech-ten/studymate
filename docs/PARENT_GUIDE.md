# StudyMate Parent Guide

## Welcome to StudyMate

StudyMate is an AI-powered learning platform designed specifically for Australian primary school students. Your child can practice maths aligned with the Victorian curriculum while receiving instant help from our AI tutor.

---

## Getting Started

### Step 1: Create Your Account

**Option A: Sign up with Google (Recommended)**
1. Go to **grademychild.com.au/get-started**
2. Click **"Continue with Google"**
3. Select your Google account
4. Choose your subscription tier
5. You're ready to go!

**Option B: Sign up with Email**
1. Go to **grademychild.com.au/get-started**
2. Enter your email and create a password
3. Check your email for a verification code
4. Enter the code to verify your account

**Already have an account?** You can add Google sign-in to your existing account at any time. Just click "Continue with Google" on the login page using the same email address. Your accounts will be automatically linked.

### Step 2: Add Your Child

1. Click **"Add Child"** from your dashboard
2. Enter your child's first name (we don't collect last names for privacy)
3. Select their year level (Year 3, 4, 5, or 6)
4. Create a 4-digit PIN for your child to log in
5. Choose an avatar together

### Step 3: Your Child Takes a Placement Test

The first time your child logs in, they'll take a short benchmark test. This helps us understand their current level so we can personalise their learning experience. The test takes about 5-10 minutes.

## How Your Child Uses StudyMate

### Logging In

1. Your child goes to **grademychild.com.au/child-login**
2. They enter their username and 4-digit PIN
3. They see their dashboard with XP, streaks, and subjects

### Learning a Topic

1. Select **Maths** from the subjects
2. Browse the curriculum: Number & Algebra, Measurement, etc.
3. Pick a topic like "Fractions" or "Multiplication"
4. Read the notes and examples
5. Take the quiz (5-10 questions)

### Getting Help

If your child gets a question wrong, they can:
- See the correct answer with an explanation
- Ask the AI tutor for more help
- Type questions like "I don't understand why 3/4 is bigger than 1/2"

The AI tutor responds at their year level using Australian examples.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How does the AI tutor work?
Our AI tutor uses the same technology as ChatGPT but is specifically trained to help Australian primary school students. It knows the Victorian curriculum and responds at an appropriate level for your child's year group.

### Is my child's data safe?
Yes. We take privacy seriously:
- We only store your child's first name and year level
- All data is stored in Australia (Sydney)
- We don't sell data or show ads to children
- You can delete your account anytime
- If you sign in with Google, we only store your email and name

### Can I sign in with Google?
Yes! We support Google Sign-In for faster access:
- Click "Continue with Google" on the login or signup page
- If you already have an email/password account, it will be automatically linked
- You can then sign in using either method
- You'll see a notification when accounts are linked

### How much time should my child spend on StudyMate?
We recommend 15-20 minutes daily. Consistency is more important than duration. The streak feature encourages regular practice without marathon sessions.

### Can I see what my child is learning?
Yes! Your dashboard shows:
- Topics they've studied
- Quiz scores
- AI tutor conversations (coming soon)
- Weekly progress summary

### My child is struggling. Will StudyMate help?
StudyMate adapts to your child's level. If they're struggling with a topic:
1. The system will show easier questions first
2. The AI tutor explains concepts in simple terms
3. They can repeat topics as many times as needed
4. There's no penalty for getting things wrong

### My child is advanced. Will StudyMate challenge them?
Yes! The adaptive system will:
1. Recognise when your child masters a topic
2. Present harder questions
3. Introduce topics above their year level
4. Offer Cambridge-style exam practice

### What's covered in the curriculum?
Currently, we cover Year 3-6 Maths including:
- Number & Algebra (place value, fractions, decimals, patterns)
- Measurement & Geometry (length, area, time, shapes, angles)
- Statistics & Probability (data, graphs, chance)

English and Science coming soon.

### Can I use this for multiple children?
Yes! The Free plan includes 2 children. Upgrade to Scholar (5 children) or Achiever (10 children) for larger families.

### What if I need help?
Contact us through the app or email support. We typically respond within 24 hours.
